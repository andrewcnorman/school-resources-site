Introduction to the DofE

You will be invited to an intoductory session in a computer room. At one of these sessions, you will learn:

  • learn about the aims of the DofE bronze award are,
  • find out school's expectations of you as a participant,
  • log into the eDofE system for the first time and fill in some details,
  • receive your welcome pack, and find out how to use it to record your progress and sign off the sections of your award.

All of the documents below should be in your welcome pack. If you need replacements for any reason, you can download them below.

  • Participant's Handbook
  • Assessor Card booklet (alternatively, your assessors can add their Assessor Reports online as described in this guide)
