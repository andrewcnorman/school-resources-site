AuroraWatch magnetometer

Bishop Heber High School is one of ten schools nationally which has been selected to host an AuroraWatch magnetometer, following a competition in which 165 schools entered.

This map shows the entrants for the AuroraWatch schools' magnetometer competition. Selected schools are shown in red. [CREDIT: AuroraWatch UK]

The ten selected schools are:

  • Brae High School, Shetland.
  • Sanday Community School, Sanday, Orkney.
  • Tobermory High School, Mull.
  • St. Benedicts High School, Whitehaven, Cumbria.
  • Altrincham Grammar School for Girls, Altrincham, Cheshire.
  • Bishop Heber High School, Malpas, Cheshire.
  • Queen Elizabeth’s Grammar School, Ashbourne, Derbyshire.
  • Shelfield, Community Academy, Pelsall, West Midlands.
  • Gwernyfed High School, Brecon, Powys.
  • Simon Langton Grammar School for Boys, Canterbury, Kent.

The schools' magnetometer data will be included on the AuroraWatch UK web pages later this year.
