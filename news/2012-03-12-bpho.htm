Physics Challenge

The BPhO Physics Challenge competition is organized nationally for pupils taking a GCSE in physics, and is designed to test their knowledge and understanding of basic physical principles. The year 11 separate scientists (i.e. those who will sit separate science exams in physics, chemistry and biology) all participated in the "Physics Challenge" competition.

The following pupils all achieved Bronze award: Poppy Burgess (11JHu), Henry Delf-Rowlandson (11GGa), Jamie Ganley (11GGa), Felix Moriss (11NWa), Becky Ralph (11KCo), Ciaran O'Shea (11GGa), Matthew Sinclair (11KCo), Sam Tute (11KCo), Harry Young (11JHu).
