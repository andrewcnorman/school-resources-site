Rutherford School Physics Project

The Rutherford School Physics Project is an initiative—led by the University of Cambridge—to offer support and activities in physics problem solving to students transitioning from GCSE (Y11), through to Sixth Form (Y12 & 13), through to university. Further information is available from the links below.
