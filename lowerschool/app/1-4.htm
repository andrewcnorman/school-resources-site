AF1.4

AF1 — Thinking scientifically

Thread 4 — Provisional nature of scientific evidence

Level 8
Analyse the development of scientific theories through the emergence of new, accepted ideas and evidence.
Level 7
Explain how different pieces of evidence support accepted scientific ideas or contribute to questions that science cannot fully answer.
Level 6
Describe some scientific evidence that supports or refutes particular ideas or arguments, including those in development.
Level 5
Recognise scientific questions that do not yet have definitive answers
Level 4
Identify scientific evidence that is being used to support or refute ideas or arguments.
