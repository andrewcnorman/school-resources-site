AF1.3

AF1 — Thinking scientifically

Thread 3 — The process of development of scientific ideas including the role of the scientific community in their development

Level 8
Analyse the development of scientific theories through the emergence of new, accepted ideas and evidence.
Level 7
Explain the processes by which ideas and evidence are accepted or rejected by the scientific community
Level 6
Explain how new scientific evidence is discussed and interpreted by the scientific community and how this may lead to changes in scientific ideas.
Level 5
Identify the use of evidence and creative thinking by scientists in the development of scientific ideas
Level 4
Use scientific ideas when describing simple processes or phenomena
Level 3
Respond to ideas given to them to answer questions or suggest solutions to problems.
