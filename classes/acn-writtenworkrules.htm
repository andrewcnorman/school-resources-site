Mr. Norman's
Rules for written work

  1. All written work will be in best possible handwriting. Pen & ink is preferred. Good quality alternatives are acceptable.
  2. All written work will be given a heading and a date. These will be underlined with straight lines.
  3. Great care will be taken with grammar, spelling and punctuation.
  4. Mistakes will be corrected neatly.
  5. Great care will be taken of exercise books. They will not become 'battered' or 'dog-eared'. They will be kept away from tea, coffee, toast, butter, jam, pet animals and younger brothers or sisters.
  6. All written work will be a delight for Mr. Norman to look at.
