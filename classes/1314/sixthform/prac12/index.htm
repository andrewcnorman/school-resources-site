Practical Physics

Practical physics is often sadly neglected in UK schools. It is a very important part of the subject, as it is how we test theory and gain information about the universe.

You have to read over each practical (best done the night before) and complete a reading memo on it, just like for a theory lesson. You will be assigned a practical partner and a practical to conduct for each practical lesson (these ought to be about once per fortnight, as arranged).

Autumn Term Circus

There are six practicals in the autumn circus, and they are about particle physics, current electricity and quantum phenomena.
