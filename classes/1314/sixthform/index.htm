Careers in physics

  • What sorts of degree courses / careers can an A-level in physics be useful in?
  • What careers might a physics degree lead to?
  • Informed choices [PDF] - a guide on choosing A-levels from the Russell group, see particularly pp. 25–27 on "facilitating subjects" (physics is one), p. 30 on science courses, and pp. 35-47 on A-levels for individual degree courses
  • Physics and: its advantages [PDF] - an IOP briefing note on the importance of getting more people to study physics at A-level and beyond in the UK (2011)
  • physics.org careers - a good place to start thinking about what careers are available with a physics degree.
  • The Career Paths of Physics Graduates [PDF, 1 MB] - an IOP report based on a study conducted in 2006–2010
  • Once a physicist - profiles of people who have a physics degree, but who now do something outside of physics. They all talk about how physics has been important in their careers.
