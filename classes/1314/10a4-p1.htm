AQA glossary

I strongly suggest you make sure that you know what the following words mean before next Tuesday.

independent variable
something you change
dependent variable
the thing you measure
control variable
something you keep the same
resolution
the smallest division on a measuring device
range
the highest value take away the lowest value
hypothesis
a suggested answer you want to test
conclusion
summing up what you have found out
