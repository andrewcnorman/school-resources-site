Gravitation

Newton's law of gravitation has been called the greatest generalization achieved by the human mind. It is equally remarkable that nature can follow such an elegantly simple principle. We shall learn about Newton's law, and also get an elementary understanding of how fields can be a helpful concept in physics.

Course outline

  • Newton's law of gravitation
  • Gravitational fields
  • Orbits
  • Gravitational potential energy
  • Equipotentials and potential
