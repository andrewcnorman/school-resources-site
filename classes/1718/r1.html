General information

At the end of the year, you will have an exam in physics on everything which you have learnt this year. Provided that you keep up-to-date with work and try quite hard during the year, it should not require much special preparation beyond jogging your memory of all that we have covered, nor prove especially difficult. We shall discuss it further nearer the time.

You are the first year at Radley to study for separate IGCSE exams in Physics, Chemistry and Biology. This means there is a little bit more material to cover in the remove, and so the course will progress quite quickly. If at any time you feel you need some extra help on a particular point or topic, please feel free to come along to my office hours.
