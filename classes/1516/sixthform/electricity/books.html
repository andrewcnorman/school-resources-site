Recommended Books

In addition to the textbooks provided by the school, you might like to extend your reading by dipping into the following books:

  • Newton, Isaac, Opticks, £8, Dover ISBN 0-486-60205-2
  • Hecht, Eugene Optics, Addison Wesley - More advanced than what we need, but beautifully illustrated
  • M Minnaert, translated by H M Kremer-Priest and revised by K E B Jay, The Nature of Light and Colour in the Open Air, £16.99 ISBN 0 486 20196 1

Happy reading!
