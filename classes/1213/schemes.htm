Year 11

The year 11s are the first year group to follow the AQA's GCSE science course.

This is the triple science group, who will study for three separate GCSEs in physics, chemistry and biology. Their physics GCSE at the end of year 11 will comprise three units of physics, plus an ISA controlled assessment in physics (and similarly in chemistry and biology).
